Top tier tofu recipe: Crispy Honey Garlic Tofu

Tofu is a scary food for anyone to try. I'll keep it real, I don't really like tofu. However, certain ways to prepare tofu make it taste fantastic. This recipe is one of my favorites, as the sauce is phenomenal.

Crispy tofu nuggets

  • 400g tofu, cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp oil for frying (olive or oil preference)

Honey garlic souce

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 6 green onion, thinly slices (white and green parts separated)
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
